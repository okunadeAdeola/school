ADEOLA UNIVERSITY

Adeola University of Technology (ADETECH) is a prestigious tertiary institution located in Ogbomoso, Oyo State, Nigeria. Established in 1990, the university was named after Chief Okunade Adeola, a prominent Nigerian citizen. ADETECH was founded through the collaboration of two Nigerian states: Oyo and Osun, hence its joint ownership.
